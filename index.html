Welcome to Malay bank . we are one of the biggest bank in malaysia . we are ready to serve you unconditionally.

Established in 1998, Malay Bank has grown from a small regional bank in Kuala Lumpur into one of Malaysia’s most trusted financial institutions. For over two decades, we have been committed to providing secure, reliable, and innovative banking solutions to individuals, businesses, and communities across the nation.

Our journey began with a simple mission – to make banking easier and more accessible for everyone. Over the years, we have introduced modern banking technologies such as online banking, mobile banking apps, and instant fund transfers, all while maintaining the personal touch that our customers value.

Today, Malay Bank operates across Malaysia with multiple branches and ATM networks, offering services ranging from savings and current accounts to personal, business, and housing loans. Our focus on customer care, transparency, and community development continues to drive our success.

At Malay Bank, we believe in more than just numbers – we believe in building a brighter financial future for every Malaysian.

Student Account

The Student Account is designed for students aged 18–25, offering zero maintenance fees, easy online banking, and exclusive student benefits.

  • Eligibility: Must be a registered student in Malaysia.
  • Minimum Deposit: RM50
  • Monthly Fee: RM0
  • Special Benefits: Free ATM withdrawals nationwide, discounts at partner bookstores, and online study resources.

Whether you’re managing your allowance or part-time job income, the Student Account keeps your finances simple and accessible.

Saving Account

Our Savings Account helps you grow your money with attractive interest rates while keeping your funds accessible anytime.

  • Eligibility: Malaysian citizens or residents, 18 years and above.
  • Minimum Deposit: RM100
  • Interest Rate: 1.5% – 2.0% p.a.
  • Benefits: Free online banking, ATM access, and e-statements.

Fixed Deposit (FDR)

A Fixed Deposit Account lets you lock in your savings for a fixed term with guaranteed returns at higher interest rates.

  • Eligibility: Malaysian citizens or permanent residents, 18+
  • Minimum Deposit: RM1,000
  • Tenure Options: 1 month to 5 years
  • Interest Rate: Up to 3.5% p.a.

Pension Account

The Pension Account is tailored for retirees, offering monthly interest payouts and special benefits for senior citizens.

  • Eligibility: Retired individuals aged 55 and above.
  • Minimum Deposit: RM500
  • Benefits: Higher interest rates, priority customer service, and free cheque books.

Micro Loan

Micro Loans are small-scale financing solutions for small businesses, startups, and self-employed individuals.

  • Loan Amount: RM500 – RM20,000
  • Tenure: 6 months to 3 years
  • Interest Rate: From 5% p.a.

Car Loan

Finance your dream vehicle with our flexible car loan plans and low interest rates.

  • Loan Amount: Up to 90% of car value

Home Loan
